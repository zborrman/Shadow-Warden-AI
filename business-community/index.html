◈ SEP Entity Identity

Every entity gets a
cryptographic passport

UECIIDs (Unified Entity Community Identity IDs) encode a 64-bit Snowflake timestamp into 11 base-62 characters. The result is sortable, URL-safe, and globally unique across all federated communities.

Snowflake epoch 2024-01-01T00:00:00Z · microsecond precision
Base-62 alphabet 0-9A-Za-z · case-sensitive · 11 characters
Lexicographic order equals chronological order — no secondary sort needed
Cross-community source UECIID preserved on peering transfer
UECIID format
SEP-0Xk4aB7mN2p
SEP- Prefix fixed 4-char namespace
0Xk4a Timestamp ≈ first 5 chars encode timestamp
B7mN2p Random last 6 chars — sequence + machine ID
11 base-62 Total 62¹¹ = 52 quadrillion IDs
