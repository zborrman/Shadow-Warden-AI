Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer a free trial?

14 days on Individual, Community Business, and Pro — no credit card required.

Can I upgrade or downgrade?

Yes. Upgrades are immediate; downgrades take effect at the next billing date. Managed through the Lemon Squeezy Customer Portal.

What payment methods do you accept?

All major credit/debit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers for Enterprise. Processed securely by Lemon Squeezy.

Is my data safe during checkout?

All payments are handled by Lemon Squeezy (PCI-DSS Level 1). Shadow Warden AI never sees your card details.

Can I get a refund?

30-day money-back guarantee on all plans. Contact support@shadow-warden-ai.com.

What happens if I exceed my request limit?

On Pro and above, requests are never blocked when you exceed your monthly limit. Planned: automatic overage billing at $0.05 per 1k additional requests (BL-19, coming soon). You will receive a Slack alert from SOVA at 80% and 100% of your limit. Enterprise plans have unlimited requests with no overage.
