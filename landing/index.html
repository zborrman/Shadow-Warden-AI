Risks We Manage

The AI Threat Landscape, Covered

Shadow Warden is purpose-built for threats that traditional security tools miss entirely.

Prompt Injection Attacks

Malicious instructions embedded in user input or retrieved data hijack your AI's behaviour. Detected by MiniLM semantic model + 300+ rules.

OWASP LLM01

AI Data Leaks

SSNs, API keys, IBANs, and credentials sent to AI providers in plain text. 17+ PII patterns stripped before transmission — GDPR-compliant by design.

OWASP LLM06

Compliance & Regulatory Risks

GDPR Art. 30, EU AI Act, and SOC 2 require demonstrable AI data controls. Shadow Warden generates per-tenant audit trails automatically.

GDPR · EU AI Act · SOC 2

Toxic Content Generation

AI models manipulated into generating harmful, discriminatory, or legally-risky content. Semantic rules detect and block before output reaches users.

OWASP LLM09

Indirect Prompt Injection

Instructions hidden inside web pages, documents, or emails hijack agents that retrieve external content. Obfuscation decoder catches base64, hex, ROT13, and homoglyph variants.

OWASP LLM01 variant

Multilingual & Multimodal Attacks

Attacks crafted in non-English languages or embedded in images bypass naive keyword filters. MiniLM semantic embeddings detect intent regardless of language or encoding.

Language-agnostic detection
