Agentic Commerce & Web3

The first AI security gateway with built-in mandate controls for AI-driven purchasing — signed on-chain, audited via STIX, compliant by design.

Spending Mandates (AP2)
Cryptographically signed Ed25519 + HMAC spending caps. AI agents can only purchase within approved limits — hard-enforced, not just monitored.
On-Chain Mandates (Web3)
Deploy mandates to Ethereum / Polygon via smart contract. IPFS metadata storage. Immutable audit trail — no one can dispute what the agent was authorised to spend.
Multi-Agent Auction
Claude, Gemini, and GPT compete to find the best vendor for your purchase request. Shadow Warden scores each proposal on price, delivery, and supplier risk.
Tax & Invoice Engine
Automatic VAT/GST calculation for 50+ jurisdictions (EU OSS, US Sales Tax, UK VAT, SG GST). ReportLab PDF invoices stored in MinIO for every order.
