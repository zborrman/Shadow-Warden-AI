Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026  ·  Effective: March 19, 2026

Privacy by Design: Shadow Warden AI never logs or stores the content of AI requests — only anonymized metadata. Your data does not leave your infrastructure.

1. Who We Are

Shadow Warden AI Ltd. ("Shadow Warden", "we", "us") operates the Shadow Warden AI security gateway. We are the data controller for information collected through our website and Service.

Contact: privacy@shadow-warden-ai.com · Tel Aviv, Israel

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Account Data

  • Name, email address, and company (provided on signup)
  • Billing information (processed by Lemon Squeezy — we never see raw card data)
  • API keys (stored as SHA-256 hashes, never in plaintext)

2.2 Service Metadata (not content)

When the Service processes AI requests, we log only:

2.3 Website Analytics

We collect standard server logs (IP address, user-agent, page URL, referrer) to maintain and improve the website. These are retained for 30 days.

3. How We Use Data

  • Service delivery — authenticating requests, enforcing rate limits, routing API calls
  • Billing and subscription management — via Lemon Squeezy
  • Security analytics — aggregate threat statistics, model improvement (using only anonymized risk labels, never content)
  • Legal compliance — fulfilling GDPR data subject requests, responding to lawful legal process
  • Product communications — service notices, security alerts, changelog (you may unsubscribe at any time)

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We share data only with:

  • Lemon Squeezy — payment processing and tax compliance
  • Cloud infrastructure providers — hosting (servers located in EU/US regions)
  • Law enforcement — only when required by valid legal process

6. Data Retention

  • Service metadata logs: 90 days by default (configurable per tenant down to 7 days)
  • Account data: retained for the duration of your subscription + 30 days after termination
  • Billing records: 7 years (legal/tax requirement)
  • GDPR export/purge available via the API (POST /gdpr/purge) or by emailing privacy@shadow-warden-ai.com

7. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Depending on your jurisdiction, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of your data (POST /gdpr/export)
  • Rectification — correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure — delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability — receive your data in machine-readable format
  • Object — opt out of processing based on legitimate interests
  • Withdraw consent — unsubscribe from marketing at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@shadow-warden-ai.com. We respond within 30 days.

8. Security

We apply industry-standard security measures including TLS 1.3 in transit, encryption at rest, API key hashing (SHA-256), and non-root container execution. No system is 100% secure — report suspected vulnerabilities to security@shadow-warden-ai.com.

9. Cookies

Our website uses only essential session cookies (authentication token in localStorage). We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

10. International Transfers

Your data may be processed in the EU or US. Where transfers occur, we rely on Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission.

11. Children

The Service is not directed to individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

12. Changes

We will notify you by email 30 days before material changes take effect. The "last updated" date at the top of this page always reflects the current version.

13. Contact & Supervisory Authority

Privacy enquiries: privacy@shadow-warden-ai.com

You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. For EU residents, a list of authorities is available at edpb.europa.eu.
