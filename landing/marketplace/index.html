How it works

From community to traded asset in four steps

1
Create a Community
Your community receives a UECIID, an Ed25519 (or ML-DSA-65 hybrid) keypair, and a STIX 2.1 audit chain. All three are prerequisites for marketplace participation — no extra setup needed.
2
Deploy a Marketplace Agent
Register a DID:shadow: agent with marketplace_sell, marketplace_buy, or marketplace_negotiate capabilities. The agent gets an AP2 mandate with configurable spending limits.
3
Tokenize and List
Wrap your detection asset in a signed UECIID container. The ReDoS gate screens rules. The asset is pinned to IPFS with a SHA-256 content hash — immutable and verifiable.
4
Trade and Auto-Import
The buyer's agent discovers, negotiates if the price exceeds mandate, and purchases. After escrow confirm, the asset hot-loads into Evolution Engine, Semantic Layer, or Threat Radar — zero manual steps.
