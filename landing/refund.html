1. 14-Day Money-Back Guarantee

If you are not satisfied with Shadow Warden AI for any reason within 14 calendar days of your first payment on a new Pro or MSP subscription, you may request a full refund of that first payment.

  • The guarantee applies to your first billing period only.
  • Renewals and subsequent billing periods are not eligible for the money-back guarantee.
  • Only one money-back claim per customer per lifetime is permitted.

2. Eligibility

A refund request is eligible if all of the following are true:

  • The request is submitted within 14 days of the original payment date.
  • The subscription is Pro or MSP (Free plan has no charge and is ineligible).
  • The account has not violated our Terms of Service.
  • The account has not already received a refund from us.

3. Service Credits for Outages

For Pro and MSP plans, if monthly uptime falls below 99.9%, you may request a pro-rated service credit for the affected hours. Credits are applied to your next invoice and cannot be exchanged for cash.

To claim a credit, email billing@shadow-warden-ai.com within 30 days of the outage with your tenant ID and approximate time range.

4. Non-Refundable Situations

  • Subscription renewals after the initial 14-day period.
  • Accounts terminated for Terms of Service violations.
  • Partial months where you elected to cancel mid-cycle (access continues until the billing period ends).
  • Charges for overages or add-ons beyond your plan's included quota.
  • Free plan — no charges apply.

5. How to Request a Refund

Email billing@shadow-warden-ai.com with:

  • Subject line: Refund Request — [your account email]
  • Your registered email address
  • The Paddle transaction ID (found in your billing confirmation email)
  • Brief reason for the request (optional but helps us improve)

We will respond within 2 business days. Approved refunds are processed through Paddle and typically appear on your statement within 5–10 business days depending on your bank or card issuer.

6. Cancellations

You may cancel your subscription at any time from the billing portal (shadow-warden-ai.com/billing) or by emailing billing@shadow-warden-ai.com. After cancellation:

  • Your access and API keys remain active until the end of the current billing period.
  • You will not be charged for the next period.
  • No partial refund is issued for the remaining days in the current period.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy at any time. The version in effect at the time of your purchase governs your refund eligibility. We will notify existing subscribers of material changes 30 days in advance via email.
